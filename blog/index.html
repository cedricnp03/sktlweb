ISHIDA, Kensuke (M1) receives poster presentation award
Aditya Wibawa Sakti Principal Investigator Waseda University
October 11, 2023 September 26, 2024
misc

Congratulations to Kensuke Ishida, our first year master course student who won the first winner of the poster competition in the International Conference on Chemistry and Material Science 2023. The conference was held in Malang, Indonesia. Ishida-san presented the poster entitled, “Quantum Molecular Simulations for Lithium-Ion Transport in Solid Polymer Electrolyte of Layered Cellulose and Its Derivatives”.
